All-in-one Cheese sauce recipe

The recipe is very quick to make and you just add everything together(except cheese if your using) in a pan and gently whisk

Ingredients

  • 570ml milk
  • 40g plain wholemeal flour
  • 40g butter
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 70g blue cheese (can use cheddar or other similar cheese if you prefer)
  • a little nutmeg
  • salt and pepper to taste
