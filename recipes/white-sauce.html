All-in-one Blue cheese sauce recipe

The recipe is very quick and easy to make. The blue cheese sauce goes well with sweet and slightly spicy flavours.

Ingredients

  • 570ml milk
  • 40g plain wholemeal flour
  • 40g butter
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 110g blue cheese (I use stilton but you can use mature cheddar or other similar strong cheese)
  • a little nutmeg
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Put milk,butter, flour and cayenne pepper in a medium pan and place it over a gentle heat
  2. Then, using a balloon whisk, begin to whisk while bringing it to a gentle simmer.
  3. Whisk continually until you have a smooth, glossy sauce, and simmer very gently for 5 minutes.
  4. Then add the cheese and whisk again, allowing it to melt.
  5. Then season with salt and pepper and some freshly grated nutmeg.
