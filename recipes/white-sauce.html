All-in-one Blue cheese sauce recipe

Description

The recipe is very quick and easy to make. The blue cheese sauce goes well with sweet and slightly spicy flavours. I use stilton but you can use another blue cheese or any other hardish cheese or a mix of cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 570ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 40g plain wholemeal flour
  • 40g butter
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 110g stilton cheese (you can use any blue cheese or similar strong cheese)
  • a little nutmeg
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add milk,butter, flour and cayenne pepper to a medium pan and place it over a gentle heat
  2. Then, using a balloon whisk, begin to whisk while bringing it to a gentle simmer.
  3. Whisk continually until you have a smooth, glossy sauce, and simmer very gently for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cheese and whisk again, allowing it to melt.
  5. Then season with salt and pepper and some freshly grated nutmeg.
