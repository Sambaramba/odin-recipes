Easy Vegetable and Paneer Korma recipe

Description

This is an easy vegetable and paneer Korma recipe. Indian/south asian cusine is some of my favourite foods as I find the meals comforting, not too unhealthy and I love the spice combinations. This Korma is full of vegetables and spices for nutrients and has paneer for a decent main protein source.

Ingredients
