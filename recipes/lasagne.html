Lasagne

Description

This is an easy vegetarian lasagne recipe that uses lentils for the subsitute protein source. The combination of blue cheese with tomatoes is a nice contast and the veggies and lentils makes it fairly healthy. The recipe is also pretty versatile as you can use whatever veggies you like and put a variety of cheeses in the sauce.

The recipe serves 4-6 people. It takes approximately 25 minutes to prepare and a further 45-50 minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 350g lasagne sheets
  • 125g dried red,brown or green lentils(400g cooked lentils if using tin)

For Roasted vegetables

  • 1 large courgette, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 2 peppers, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 1 small sweet potato,cut into 2 cm/ ¾ inch chunks
  • 1 small garlic bulb, broken into cloves with ends chopped off and skin still on
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 4 tbsp cold pressed rapeseed oil
  • ½ tin of chopped tomatoes or ¼ quanity left over tomato sauce

Optional extras

  • 80g olives, chopped
  • 70g Sundried tomatoes, chopped
  • 80g Artichokes,chopped in half
  • 250g mushrooms, chopped in half

For the blue cheese sauce

  • 1 quantity of all-in-one blue cheese sauce

Method

  1. Wash,soak and cook lentils
  2. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C Fan/425°F (gas mark 7)
  3. Whilst the lentils are cooking prep vegatables, put in roasting dish/on bakin sheets with tray then place in oven for 35-40 mins
  4. After 25 minutes check vegetables and take out any that are done.
  5. When vegetables are done take out of oven,add tomato sauce/chopped tomatos and lentils then mix to combine. If using (and not already in sauce) add olives and sun-dried tomatos
  6. Whilst the vegetables are cooking you can prepare the blue cheese sauce following these instructions.
