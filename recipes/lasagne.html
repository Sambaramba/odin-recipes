Lasagne

Description

This is an easy vegetarian lasagne recipe that uses lentils for the subsitute protein source. The combination of blue cheese with tomatoes is a nice contast and the veggies and lentils makes it fairly healthy. The recipe is also pretty versatile as you can use change the roasted veggies to suit your taste,use left over tomato sauce instead of chopped toms, and put a variety of cheeses in the sauce..

The recipe serves 4-6 people. It takes approximately 25 minutes to prepare and a further 45-50 minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

For Roasted vegetables

  • 125g dried lentils/400g can lentils
  • 1 large courgette, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 2 yellow or red peppers, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 1 small sweet potato,cut into 2 cm/ ¾ inch chunks
  • 1 red chilli, seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 1 garlic bulb, 3 cloves finely chopped, the rest with ends chopped off and skin still on
  • 4 tbsp cold pressed rapeseed oil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes or ¼ quanity left over tomato sauce
  • 1 stock cube/2 teaspoons stock powder
  • 100g young spinach/ 50g frozen spinach (thawed)
  • 9-10 lasagne sheets
  • salt and grounded pepper

Optional extras

  • 80g olives, chopped in half
  • 70g Sundried tomatoes, chopped

For the blue cheese sauce

  • 1 quantity of all-in-one blue cheese sauce

Method

  1. Wash,soak and cook lentilsaccording to instructions
  2. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C Fan/425°F (gas mark 7)
  3. Whilst the lentils are cooking prepare and roast the vegetables. After preparing the vegetables put them in a roasting dish/on baking trays,coat with oil and seasoning then place in oven for 35-40 mins (check them after 25 mins)
  4. While the vegetables are in the oven,heat the remaining oil in a medium sized pan and gently fry the onion for 5 minutes,stirring regularly.
  5. Add the chilli and garlic and cook for another minute,then stir in the tomatos,herbs and crumbled stock cube. Bring to the boil then simmer for 20 minutes until the sauce has reduced a bit. Season sauce at the end and balsamic vinegar if using.
  6. When vegetables are done take out of oven,add tomato sauce/chopped tomatos and lentils then mix to combine. If using (and not already in sauce) add olives and sun-dried tomatos
  7. When the tomato sauce is simmering you can prepare the blue cheese sauce following these instructions.
  8. Turn the oven down to 180°C/160°C fan/350°F(gas mark 4).
