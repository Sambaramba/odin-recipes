Lasagne

Description

This is an easy vegetarian lasagne recipe that uses lentils for the subsitute protein source. The combination of blue cheese with tomatoes is a nice contast and the veggies and lentils makes it fairly healthy. The recipe is also pretty versatile as you can use whatever veggies you like and put a variety of cheeses in the sauce. The recipe serves 4-6 people. It takes approximately 25 minutes to prepare and a further 45-50 minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 350g lasagne sheets
  • 125g dried red,brown or green lentils(400g cooked lentils if using tin)

For Roasted vegetables

  • 1 large courgette, cut into 2.5cm/inch chunks
  • 2 peppers, cut into 2.5 cm/inch chunks
  • 1 red onion, chopped into 2.5cm/inch chunks
  • 1 small garlic bulb, broken into cloves with ends chopped off and skin still on
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 4 tbsp cold pressed rapeseed oil
  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes or 1/4 quanity left over tomato sauce

Optional extras

  • 80g olives, chopped
  • 70g Sundried tomatoes, chopped
  • 80g Artichokes,chopped in half
  • 250g mushrooms, chopped in half

For the blue cheese sauce

  • 1 quantity of all-in-one blue cheese sauce

Method

  1. Wash,soak and cook lentils
  2. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C Fan/425°F (gas mark 7)
  3. Whilst the lentils are cooking,prep vegatables and put in roasting tray/on bakin sheets with tray
