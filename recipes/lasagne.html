Lasagne

Description

This is an easy vegetarian lasagne recipe where I have swapped the meat for lentils whilst still keeping it packed full of flavour. The recipe serves 6-8 people. It takes approximately 25 minutes to prepare and a further 45-50 minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

For the roasted vegetable and lentil tomato sauce

  • 1 large courgette, cut into 2.5cm/inch chunks
  • 2 peppers, cut into 2.5 cm/inch chunks
  • 1 red onion, chopped into 2.5cm/inch chunks
  • 1 small garlic bulb, broken into cloves with skin on
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 tbsp cold pressed rapeseed oil
  • 400g tin chopped tomatos
  • 125g dried lentils, washed,soaked and cooked

Optional extras for tomato sauce

  • 80g olives, chopped
  • 70g Sundried tomatoes, chopped
  • 80g Artichokes,chopped in half
  • 250g mushrooms, chopped in half

For the white sauce

  • 40g butter
  • 40g plain flour
  • 570ml milk
  • pinch freshly grated nutmeg
  • 70g cheese (I use either cheddar or blue cheese)
