Roasted vegetable,lentil and blue cheese Lasagne

Description

This is a basic vegetarian lasagne recipe that uses lentils for the main protein source. The combination of blue cheese with tomatoes is a nice flavour contrast and the veggies and lentils makes it fairly well balanced and healthy. The recipe is also pretty versatile as you can use change the roasted veggies to suit your taste,use left over tomato sauce instead of chopped tomatoes, and put a variety of cheeses in the sauce.

The recipe serves 4-6 people. The preparation time is 20-30 mins and the total cooking time is 1-2 hours.

Ingredients

For the roasted vegetables and lentil tomato sauce

  • 125g dried lentils/400g can lentils,washed,soaked and cooked.
  • 1 large courgette, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 2 yellow or red peppers, cut into 2 cm/¾ inch chunks
  • 1 medium carrot,chopped into 1cm/½ inch chunks.
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped.
  • 3 tbsp cold pressed rapeseed oil
  • 1 garlic bulb, 3 cloves finely chopped, the rest with ends chopped off and skin still on.
  • 1 red chilli, seeds removed and finely chopped
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes or ½ quanity left over tomato sauce
  • 1 stock cube/2 tsp stock powder
  • 100g young spinach/ 70g frozen spinach (thawed)
  • 9-10 wholewheat lasagne sheets
  • salt and grounded pepper
  • 50g reduced fat cheddar,grated.

Optional extras

  • 80g olives, chopped in half
  • 70g Sundried tomatoes, chopped

For the blue cheese sauce

  • 1 quantity of all-in-one blue cheese sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the lentils, then cook according to the instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 220°C/200°C Fan/425°F (gas mark 7)
  3. Whilst the lentils are cooking roast the vegetables. Put the courgettes,peppers,onion and garlic(except the 3 cloves for tom sauce) in a roasting dish/on baking trays,coat with 2 tbsp of the oil and seasoning then place in oven for 35-40 mins (check them after 25 mins)
  4. While the vegetables are in the oven,heat the remaining oil in a large sized pan and gently fry the onion for 5 minutes,stirring regularly.
  5. Add the chilli and remaining garlic and cook for another minute,then stir in the tomatos,herbs and crumbled stock cube/stock. Bring to the boil then simmer for 30 minutes until the sauce has reduced a bit. Add the spinach 3 minutes before the 30 minutes is up. When sauce is sufficiently reduced add balsmic vinegar and season to taste.
  6. When the tomato sauce is simmering you can prepare the blue cheese sauce following these instructions.
  7. Take vegetables out of oven ,then along with the lentils, add to the pan of tomato sauce and combine. Add olives and sun-dried tomatos too if using.
  8. Turn the oven down to 180°C/160°C fan/350°F(gas mark 4).
  9. Start building the lasagne. Spoon a third of the vegetable mixture over the bottom of a 2 ½-3 Litre/4 ½-5½ pint overnproof dish and cover with a single layer of lasagne sheets(doesn't matter if overlapping a bit). Top with another third of the vegetable mixture (doesn't matter if it doesn't cover evenly) and a second layer of lasagne sheets.
  10. Now pour over just under half of the cheese sauce and very gently top with the remaining vegetable mixture. Finish with a final layer of lasagne sheets and the rest of the cheese sauce. Sprinkle with the cheddar cheese.
  11. Put lasagne in the oven and bake for 35-40 minutes or until the pasta has softened and the topping is golden and bubbling. Stand for 5 minutes before cutting to allow the filling to settle.
