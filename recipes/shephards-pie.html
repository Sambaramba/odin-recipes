Vegetarian shephard's pie recipe

Description

This simple,hearty,vegetarian shephard's pie is packed full of healthy vegetables and lentils. Remember to use veggie worcestershire sauce and parmesan.

This recipe serves 4 people, the preparation time is less than 30 minutes and the cooking time is 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon rapeseed oil
  • 1 leek,trimmed,finely chopped
  • 2 carrots,peeled and finely chopped
  • 150g chestnut mushrooms,roughly chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves,finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried sage
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
