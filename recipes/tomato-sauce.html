Healthy tomato sauce recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a healthy vegan tomato sauce that is quite easy to make and packed full of flavour. The sauce can be eaten with pasta or used in recipes such as lasagne or canneloni. The combination of the sweetness from balsamic vinegar and the tangyness from the tomatoes is lovely.

Preparation is about 10-15 minutes and total cooking time is about 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms,sliced.
  • 1 large onion,diced
  • 6 cloves of garlic,finely sliced.
  • 3 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 2 x 400g tin chopped tomatos
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 2 tsp dried herbs (I use a mix of oregano and basil)
  • 80g Olives,cut in half (optional)
  • 70g Sundried tomatos,chopped. (optional)
  • 1 tin of cannelini beans.(optional)
  • 1 tsp Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Method

  1. Add garlic to a small pan , in a single layer, and cover with oil. Then place over a low heat for 20 mins or until just starting to turn golden.
  2. Whilst garlic is cooking. Heat a large frying pan on a medium-high heat, with a little oil. Add mushrooms in a single layer and cook for a 2-3 minutes on each side to release moisture, then take pan off heat and leave to the side.
  3. When the mushrooms have started cooking Heat a large pan on a low-medium heat, then add the onions and leave for 5 minutes to soften.
  4. Add the mushrooms and chilli to the onions and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Pour the garlic with the oil to the onions. Add sun-dried tomatos and olives as well if using. Then cook for a further 2 minutes.
  6. Add the cannelini beans,herbs and chopped tomatos and bring to the boil. Then turn down to a simmer and allow the sauce to reduce for 30 minutes (or longer if you like a thicker sauce).
  7. Add balsamic vinegar and seasoning then cook for another minute or two.
  8. Serve with pasta,greens, cheese and some sausages for a nice healthy meal
